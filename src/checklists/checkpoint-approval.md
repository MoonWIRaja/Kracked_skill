# Checklist: Checkpoint Approval

> Used at human checkpoint gates (Product Brief, PRD, Architecture, Production Deployment)

---

## Pre-Submission
- [ ] Document complete and formatted
- [ ] All required sections filled in
- [ ] No placeholder text remaining
- [ ] Consistent terminology used
- [ ] References to other documents are accurate

## Presentation
- [ ] Summary of key decisions provided
- [ ] Trade-offs clearly explained
- [ ] Risks identified and mitigations proposed
- [ ] Open questions listed (if any)
- [ ] Alternative approaches mentioned (if applicable)

## Approval States
- **APPROVED:** Proceed to next stage
- **APPROVED WITH CONDITIONS:** Proceed but address conditions
- **REVISION REQUESTED:** Revise and resubmit
- **REJECTED:** Reassess approach

## Post-Approval
- [ ] Approval recorded in document
- [ ] `status.md` updated with completed artifact
- [ ] Change log updated
- [ ] Next stage announced
